# Image attributions

These two images are generic, royalty-free placeholders used until the client
sends real photos of the ministry, the pastor, and the congregation. Both are
CC0 (public domain dedication) via Openverse, so no attribution is legally
required, but sources are recorded here for transparency.

- `ministry-bible-candle.jpg` — CC0, sourced via Openverse/rawpixel (getty
  archive). Used in the Ministry Story section.
- `coffee-fellowship.jpg` — CC0, photographer Uttkrista Chhetri, sourced via
  Openverse/WordPress Photo Directory. Used near the weekly Bible study
  schedule to echo "coffee included."

**Swap these out once real photos arrive.** Drop replacement files into this
same `public/images/` folder and update the `src` in
`src/components/MinistryStory.tsx` and `src/components/ScheduleSection.tsx`
(or ask Claude to do it — "swap in these new church photos").

No photo of a church building, congregation, or person is used anywhere on
this site. That was a deliberate choice: this is a brand-new ministry plant
with no confirmed building yet, and a stock photo of an unrelated building or
group of strangers would misrepresent this specific community. The hero uses
a plain gradient instead.
